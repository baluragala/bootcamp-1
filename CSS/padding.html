Padding

Individual Sides:

  • padding-top
  • padding-right
  • padding-bottom
  • padding-left

Example:

This div element has a padding-top:50px, padding-right:50px, padding-bottom: 50px, and padding-left:50px.

Shorthand Property:

padding: 20px 70px 85px 90px;

  • top padding is 20px
  • right padding is 70px
  • bottom padding is 85px
  • left padding is 90px

padding: 20px 70px 85px;

  • top padding is 20px
  • right and left paddings are 70px
  • bottom padding is 85px

padding: 20px 70px;

  • top and bottom paddings are20px
  • right and left paddings are 70px

padding: 20px;

  • four sides paddings are 20px
