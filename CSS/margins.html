Margins

Individual Sides:

  • margin-top
  • margin-right
  • margin-bottom
  • margin-left

Example:

This div element has a margin-top:110px, margin-right:100px, margin-bottom: 90px, and margin-left:70px.

Shorthand Property:

margin: 20px 70px 85px 90px;

  • top margin is 20px
  • right margin is 70px
  • bottom margin is 85px
  • left margin is 90px

margin: 20px 70px 85px;

  • top margin is 20px
  • right and left margins are 70px
  • bottom margin is 85px


margin: 20px 70px;

  • top and bottom margins are20px
  • right and left margins are 70px

margin: 20px;

  • four margins are 20px



Margin:auto;

Horizontally centered because it has margin: auto;

Margin:inherit

This div has an inherited left margin (from the parent div element).
